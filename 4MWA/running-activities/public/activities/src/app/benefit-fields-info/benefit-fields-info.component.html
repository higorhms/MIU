The Benefits field is meant to highlight the key advantages or gains from performing the particular running activity.

Name: A brief, clear title for the specific benefit.
Examples include "Improved Cardiovascular Fitness", "Increased Speed", etc.

Description: A brief explanation of how the running activity leads to this benefit.
For example, "Interval runs enhance your VO2 max, helping your body to use oxygen more efficiently" explains how interval runs improve cardiovascular fitness.
